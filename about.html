Our Story


Preeti Caterers has been into catering business in Solapur for more than 30 years. It all started by serving hot and delicious meal to the "Hutatma Mandir" theatre artists at 2 A.M after the shows. The overwhelming response from the theatre personalities and the local demand of homemade meals from working professionals and students, led us to start a mess. Soon we reached 200 members joining us daily.

We expanded our business by establishing Preeti Dining Hall in 1996. Today, Preeti Dining Hall is one of the most decent family restaurant in Solapur famous for Maharashtrian cuisine, culture and customs. As one of Solapur's leading caterers, we are committed to delighting our clientele with inspiring flavours, innovative concepts and an unsurpassed level of service. Our experience, knowledge, resources and team of chefs translate your event into truly special occasion with fabulous food. We have developed the capability to serve catering orders all over Maharashtra and have successfully executed few large events.

Preeti Caterers is renowned for setting new trends in catering business. Now we are proud to have a "Five Star" Catering Service in Solapur. We have charted ambitious growth plans and have launched Preeti Event Management in 2013. Our passion for catering and dynamic team of event managers and coordinators provide end to end services viz. planning, designing and managing events. We have established a reputation for creating memorable experiences for events ranging from casual parties, intimate weddings to conferences and exhibitions catering for hundreds.

With a humble beginning we have come a long way in serving our customers. The journey has been pleasant with growing base of our loyal customers. Come and taste the delicious food at our dining hall, enjoy broad range of our catering services, meticulous planning and execution of your events!
